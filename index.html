My Story

Hey there, My name is Montukeshwar Vaishnaw, and I'm a small-town guy from Katghora, Chhattisgarh, with a big passion for building things and an insatiable curiosity for how things work. After finishing my schooling at AVM Katghora, I followed my heart and decided to pursue Computer Science at MIT ADT University in Pune. The potential and ease with which we can build anything we can think of using minimal resources is what drew me to this field. After completing my education, I started working as a backend developer, but I also do freelancing on the side. I'm always looking for opportunities to push boundaries and create something new, whether it's for a client or a personal project. Besides coding, I also run a YouTube channel where I share my knowledge about tech, especially programming-related topics. I firmly believe that sharing knowledge is one of the best ways to learn and grow, and I enjoy helping others succeed in the fast-paced and ever-changing world of technology. Thank you for stopping by my personal portfolio website! Let's connect and chat more about tech. Cheers,
